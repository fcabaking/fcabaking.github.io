Our Story

Two Mexicans on a mission to share conchas with Manhattan, Kansas.

Spreading Conchas, One Shell at a Time

FCA Baking was founded by two Mexicans who wanted to bring a taste of home to the Little Apple. Conchas — those beloved Mexican sweet breads with their iconic shell pattern — are more than a pastry. They're a piece of culture, a moment of comfort, and a reminder of family tables and morning cafecito.

We bake every concha by hand using traditional recipes. Each one is made with care, from the soft, pillowy dough to the sugary vanilla or chocolate topping. No shortcuts. Just real pan dulce.

We deliver throughout Manhattan, KS. Place your order, we'll confirm with you, and then we bake fresh. Payment is simple: Cash or CashApp when we deliver.
